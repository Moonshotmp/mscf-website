About Moonshot CrossFit

A beginner-friendly CrossFit gym in Park Ridge, IL — less than 10 minutes from O'Hare Airport.

Our Story

Moonshot CrossFit was founded on a simple belief: fitness should be accessible to everyone, regardless of experience level. Too many people feel intimidated by gyms or believe they need to "get in shape" before starting CrossFit. We built Moonshot to be the opposite of that.

From day one, our focus has been on coaching — not just programming workouts, but actually teaching people how to move well, scale appropriately, and build confidence. Every member gets attention, whether it's their first class or their thousandth.

What started as a gym has grown into something bigger. Today, Moonshot CrossFit shares a building with Moonshot Medical and Performance, creating a complete ecosystem where members can train, recover with on-site physical therapy, and optimize their health with performance medicine — all under one roof.

What We Believe

Everyone Belongs

CrossFit isn't just for elite athletes. Every workout is scalable, and every person who walks through our doors is welcomed — no matter their starting point.

Coaching Matters

A good coach is the difference between getting hurt and getting results. Our team is trained to meet you where you are and help you progress safely.

Community Wins

The people you train with become the reason you show up. Our members support each other, celebrate wins together, and make fitness something you look forward to.

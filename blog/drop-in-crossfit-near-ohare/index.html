By Moonshot CrossFit · February 2026 · 5 min read

You want a real workout, with a barbell, a pull-up bar, and people who actually care about the hour they're spending in the gym.

Good news: Moonshot CrossFit is less than 10 minutes from O'Hare International Airport, and we welcome drop-ins. Here's everything you need to know.

What to Expect as a Drop-In

If you've done CrossFit before, you'll feel right at home. If you haven't, don't worry — our coaches scale every workout, and nobody's going to throw you into the deep end without a lifeline.

Here's the general flow:

  1. Show up 10-15 minutes early. This gives you time to sign a waiver, meet your coach, and get oriented. We'll ask about your experience level and any injuries.
  2. Warm-up together. Every class starts with a group warm-up led by the coach. This gets your body ready and lets you get a feel for the space.
  3. Skill or strength portion. Most classes have a strength or skill component before the conditioning piece. Your coach will help you pick appropriate weights and scaling.
  4. The workout (WOD). This is the main event — usually 8-20 minutes of work. Your coach will explain the workout, offer scaling options, and keep an eye on your movement.
  5. Cool down and hang out. Stretch, chat, grab some water. CrossFitters are friendly people, especially to visitors. You'll probably get asked where you're from and where your home gym is.

The vibe is welcoming but not soft. We push people here. That's why they come. But "push" doesn't mean reckless — it means your coach knows when to encourage you and when to pull you back. Every workout is scalable, and we'd rather you move well at a lighter weight than sloppy at a heavy one.

How to Get Here from O'Hare

It's a straight shot. From the airport, you'll head south on Mannheim Road or hop on I-90 East and take the Cumberland exit. Either way, you're looking at about 8-10 minutes depending on traffic. Plug 542 Busse Hwy, Park Ridge, IL into your GPS and it'll take you right to the door.

We're close to pretty much anywhere you'd be staying in the O'Hare corridor:

  • Rosemont — 5-8 minutes away. If you're near the convention center or the entertainment district, we're practically next door.
  • Des Plaines — 5-10 minutes. Several hotels along Mannheim and Touhy are a quick drive.
  • Niles — about 10 minutes east. Easy access via Touhy or Dempster.
  • Edison Park — the Chicago neighborhood right next to Park Ridge. Some of our members live there. 5 minutes tops.
  • Elk Grove Village / Schiller Park — 10-15 minutes. Lots of corporate hotels in this corridor.

What If I'm Not a CrossFitter?

That's fine. If you're someone who just wants a good workout while you're in town and you've never done CrossFit, you're still welcome to drop in. Just let us know ahead of time so we can pair you with the right class and give you a little extra coaching attention.

We also offer free intros if you want to sit down with a coach one-on-one first. That's usually more relevant for locals thinking about joining, but if you're curious about CrossFit and want a low-pressure introduction, it's there.

The options within Edison Park itself are limited. And the ones that do exist tend to be generic. Rows of treadmills. A rack of dumbbells. Maybe a group fitness class that is the same thing every week. Nobody knows your name. Nobody notices if you stop coming. That is the standard model, and if it worked for you, you would not be reading this.

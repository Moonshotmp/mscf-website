Moonshot CrossFit in Park Ridge is just minutes from Norwood Park and Chicago's northwest side. Expert coaching, beginner-friendly classes, and a community that keeps you coming back.

Right Around the Corner

If you're in Norwood Park, Oriole Park, or Harwood Heights, you're closer to Moonshot CrossFit than you think. Our gym at 542 Busse Hwy in Park Ridge is typically a 5-minute drive from the Norwood Park neighborhood.

We serve a lot of members from Chicago's northwest side who were looking for a real coaching-focused gym without the big-box feel. At Moonshot, every class is led by a certified coach who scales the workout to your ability. You don't need experience — just show up.

Beyond group classes, we offer personal training for adults, a kids program for ages 5-10, and teen athlete prep for ages 11-18. The whole family can train here.

One Building. One Mission.

When we opened Moonshot CrossFit, we saw how training transformed our members — not just physically, but in confidence, energy, and how they showed up in life. But we also saw the gaps.

Members would get injured and disappear for months. Others would hit plateaus they couldn't break through with training alone. Some were doing everything "right" but still felt stuck — tired, slow to recover, unable to lose weight despite consistent effort.

That's why we built Moonshot Medical and Performance in the same building. And recently, we brought physical therapy in-house by partnering with Michael from Principle Performance Health.

Now, whether you need coaching, rehab, or medical optimization — you don't have to leave the building.

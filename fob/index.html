FAQ

I'm already a class member — does my plan change?

No. Class memberships continue exactly as today on Wodify. Buying the fob add-on stacks 24/7 access on top of what you already pay — nothing about your class plan changes.

What counts as a couple?

Two adults at the same address with linked Wodify accounts. You'll enter both names and emails at checkout. Each person gets their own fob — sharing one fob between two people is not allowed.

What happens after I pay?

You'll get an emailed receipt and confirmation immediately. Once the fob system is installed, we'll reach out to schedule fob pickup and a quick orientation. Your annual term begins the day your fob is activated, not the day you pay.

What if I bring someone in or share my fob?

Fob access is revoked. No refund for the remainder of the term. Cameras are on 24/7 specifically to enforce this — it's the only way the program works for everyone.

What if the program doesn't launch?

Full refund. The Founders pre-pay locks your slot at the lowest price — but if for any reason we don't get the fob system installed, every dollar comes back.

What's "Founder status" actually?

Permanent recognition as a Founding Member of the Moonshot 24/7 program — name on the wall, first dibs on future capacity decisions, and the founder rate locked for renewal in year two.

Ready to Lock In Your Spot?

Founder slots are first-come, first-served. Once they're gone, they're gone.

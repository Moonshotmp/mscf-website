2. 24/7 Self-Directed Use

I understand that 24/7 key fob access provides entry to the Facility outside of coached class hours, when no coach, trainer, staff member, or other supervisor will be present. I acknowledge that I am training entirely on my own and that no one will be available to demonstrate movements, correct form, spot lifts, provide medical assistance, or respond to emergencies in real time. I represent that I am physically capable of engaging in unsupervised exercise, that I have either consulted a physician or freely chosen not to do so, and that I assume all risks associated with self-directed training without supervision.

3. Medical Representation

I represent that I am in good physical condition and have no medical condition (cardiovascular, musculoskeletal, neurological, or otherwise) that would prevent me from engaging in strenuous exercise. If my condition changes, I will notify Moonshot CrossFit and discontinue training as appropriate. I agree to seek immediate medical attention for any injury or illness I experience.

I agree to INDEMNIFY AND HOLD HARMLESS the Released Parties from any loss, liability, damage, or cost, including reasonable attorneys' fees, that they may incur due to my participation in Activities or my presence at the Facility, whether caused by my negligence or otherwise.

6. Fob Use & Security

I acknowledge and agree to the following non-negotiable rules of fob access:

(a) One person, one key. My fob is personal and non-transferable. I will not share my fob, lend it, or allow anyone else to use it.
(b) No guests during fob hours. I will not bring any unauthorized person into the Facility, propping the door for others, or otherwise allow anyone to enter using my access.
(c) Cameras recording 24/7. I consent to being recorded by surveillance cameras throughout the Facility at all times for security and rule enforcement.
(d) Self-directed training only. No coaching, no group instruction, no third-party training is permitted during fob hours.
(e) Personal training takes priority. If a coach is conducting a paid PT session and asks me to relocate, I will move immediately.
(f) Etiquette. I will wipe down equipment, return weights and equipment to their proper storage, and leave the Facility clean.
(g) Violation = revocation. Violation of any of the above results in immediate revocation of fob access with no refund of any prepaid amount.

7. Lost or Stolen Fob

I will report a lost or stolen fob immediately. A replacement fee of $50 applies to all replacement fobs. I am responsible for any unauthorized access that occurs prior to my report of loss.

8. Photo & Video Release

I grant Moonshot CrossFit permission to use any photographs, video recordings, or surveillance footage in which I appear for security, marketing, social media, and promotional purposes, without compensation, in perpetuity. I understand that I may revoke marketing/promotional consent in writing at any time, but security/surveillance use will continue regardless.

9. Term, Renewal & Refunds

My annual term begins on the date my fob is activated, not the date of payment. The annual fee is non-refundable except (a) if Moonshot CrossFit cancels the program prior to my fob activation, in which case I receive a full refund, or (b) at the sole discretion of Moonshot CrossFit. The term does not auto-renew; I will be notified prior to expiration with renewal options.

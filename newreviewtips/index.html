Thanks for Being Here

Your review helps others find the right gym — and helps us keep building a better community. It only takes a minute.

Snap a Quick Photo First

Before you write your review, take a photo of the gym, your workout, or a selfie with your crew. Reviews with photos get significantly more visibility and help people see what we're all about.

Tips for a Great Review

Mention your coach or favorite class

Naming a coach or class type helps others know what to expect and helps us recognize our team.

Share a specific detail

What made you join? What keeps you coming back? How has it helped you? Specifics help more than generic praise.

Don't want to type? Use voice-to-text

Tap the microphone icon on your keyboard and just talk. It's faster and usually comes out more natural.

Attach your photo

When the Google review opens, tap the camera icon to add the photo you just took. Photos make your review stand out.

How to Attach a Photo to Your Review

1

Tap the button below to open Google Reviews

It will open directly in Google Maps or your browser.

2

Select your star rating

Tap the stars at the top of the review form.

3

Tap the camera or photo icon

You'll see it below the text box. Select the photo you just took.

4

Write your review and hit Post

That's it — thank you for taking the time.

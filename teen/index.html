Youth Sports Performance Training in Park Ridge

Give your young athlete the ultimate competitive advantage. Moonshot CrossFit’s Sports Performance Training program is an elite 8-week course designed for middle and high school athletes (ages 11+) who are serious about leveling up their game. Tailored Training for Every Athlete

Football Focus

Our training emphasizes explosive power for linemen, agility and quick cuts for skill positions, and the rotational strength and stability crucial for quarterbacks and special teams.

Wrestling Focus

We build grappling endurance, brute strength for takedowns and control, and the anaerobic capacity to dominate every period of the match without gassing out.

Basketball Focus

Program goals include increasing vertical jump height for rebounds and blocks, enhancing lateral quickness for lockdown defense, and building the full-body durability to own the court all season.

Baseball/Softball Focus

We focus on developing rotational power for hitting and throwing velocity, improving first-step quickness for stealing bases, and building the core strength to transfer energy efficiently.

Soccer Focus

Training is designed to improve change-of-direction speed, build single-leg stability for powerful strikes, and develop the game-long stamina required to cover the entire pitch.
